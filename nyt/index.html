My work

24 hours out at sea with Gazan fishermen

I designed and developed this side-scroll build for a profile of fishermen in the Gaza Strip, working with photographer Marcus Yam and editor Kate Kuo.

Image magazine landing template

As a self-initiated project, I designed a landing page template for issues of LAT’s Image Magazine under the guidance of creative director Jess de Jesus. I then programmed the template as a customizable page generator, which has been used for 4 issues thus far.

What’s on your plate?

I collaborated with a team of 3 other interns on this project, wrangling their photography and reporting into a custom build that showcases people around L.A. with interesting vanity plates.

Afghan women under the Taliban

Also under Marcus Yam and Kate Kuo, I built this fast-turnaround custom build for Yam’s photo profiles of Afghan women facing the return of the Taliban.

“The Secret” Animated Home Page

As a personal project I built this animated lead-in for a theatre company website. The animation is fully responsive, and users can scroll to move it forwards and backwards.

WNSR New School Radio

I built a single-page application for my college radio station, as a homepage and audio visualizer for student-produced media. I also designed a set of header graphics for their various shows to display on podcast platforms.

Tractatus Logico-Philosophicus Reader

This site allows readers to navigate Wittgenstein’s Tractatus, a philosophy text arranged in a multileveled hierarchy. As you read, it detects your relative position in the text and maps it to a diagram using D3.js.

Reimagine California illustrations

These illustrations ran with a series of op-eds entitled How can we reimagine California, which discussed different policy issues and visions for the future of the state.
